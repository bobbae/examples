DNA punch card!

DNA punch card. A novel approach. Always thought that other approaches like DNA fountain and Catalog DNA were not quite right. DNA Fountain code seemed to break down with different sets of data. Catalog DNA approach probably had difficulties scaling. I still believe the encoding and decoding need to employ some machine learning. A combination of DNA punch card and clustering auto-encoder may get us closer.

Many dispute this and Anders Ericsson by citing quality vs. quantity of efforts and time.
